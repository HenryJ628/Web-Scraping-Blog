What is Web Scraping?

Web scraping or web crawling is the process of writing code that creates a bot which can 'crawl' the web and 'scrap' data depending on what you tell it to do

Why is it useful?

It can automate very mundane tasks but also allow you to do online research on a scale that was impossible to do manually

What projects will I do?

I will be doing projects that show the power of web scraping. This could potentially be about ways business could use it to increase profits
